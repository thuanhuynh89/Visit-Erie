Journey through Erie Peninsula

Erie, PA

Erie Peninsula is a beautiful place to visit during the summer time. It's home to a walking trail that stretches miles, a beach, canoeing, fishing, and Waldameer theme park.

Check out all the Wildlife

Lots of kinds of fish to catch and birds to see. Some common types of fish are rock bass, yellow perch, small mouth bass, and large mouth bass.

From Tents to Great Hotels

Erie is full of wonderful places to stay. You have the ability to sleep in the outdoors in a tent, or relax like a king at a five star hotel. Here are some great hotels:

  • Lake Erie Hotel
  • Sheraton Hotel
  • Avalon Hotel

Pack Accordingly

One of most important things when it comes to traveling through the great outdoors is packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring layers of clothing
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Pack light
